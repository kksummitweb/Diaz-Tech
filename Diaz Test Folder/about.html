About DiazTech

Hi, I'm Angel

I am a 17-year-old local student building DiazTech to help my community with computer repair and troubleshooting support.

Why I started DiazTech

I enjoy working with computers and helping people fix slow or problematic devices. I started this service to gain real experience while providing useful support to the community.

How I work

I focus on being honest, respectful, and clear with every repair or recommendation. I am currently offering free diagnosis while I continue to grow.

Community first

I appreciate any advice and support from local families, students, and small businesses. Every message helps DiazTech improve and serve better.

Let's connect

If your computer is having issues, send a message and I will be happy to take a look and share what I can do to help.

Support a student business

Every project helps DiazTech grow.

By choosing DiazTech, you are supporting a motivated student who is learning, improving, and giving that same energy back into each repair.
