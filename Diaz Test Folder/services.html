What to expect

Clear process, no-pressure recommendations.

DiazTech is run by Angel, a student who focuses on learning and helping, not upselling. Every repair starts with a free diagnosis so you can decide what you want to do next.

  1. 1. Share your issue

    Send a short message about what is going wrong with your device.

  2. 2. Free diagnosis

    Angel reviews the problem, checks possible causes, and explains findings.

  3. 3. Simple options

    You receive clear options such as “fix”, “upgrade”, or “not worth it”.

Typical turnaround & pricing

  • Free diagnosis for all services
  • Most cleanups and basic fixes: usually within 1–3 days
  • Hardware upgrades: timing depends on part availability
  • Final pricing is always agreed before any repair work

Exact pricing can change based on the issue and parts needed, but you will always know the plan before continuing.
