What to expect

Simple process, no confusing tech talk.

DiazTech focuses on practical support for all kinds of tech. Whether the issue is with a phone, tablet, laptop, desktop, or a custom PC build, you will get clear options before moving forward.

  1. 1. Share the issue

    Send a message with the problem, device type, and any error messages you see.

  2. 2. Free diagnosis

    The problem is reviewed and possible causes are explained in plain language.

  3. 3. Choose an option

    You get a clear recommendation such as repair, upgrade, or leave it as is.

  4. 4. Approve the plan

    No repair continues until you understand the next step and agree to it.

Typical turnaround

  • Free diagnosis for all services
  • Most software cleanups: usually within 1–3 days
  • Hardware upgrades depend on part availability
  • Pricing is confirmed before any repair work begins

Turnaround and final cost can vary by issue, but DiazTech keeps the process simple and transparent.
